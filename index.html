Hwu am I?

Hello there! My name is Kenny Hwu, and I am a highly inquisitive and curious student of the world, always interested in tinkering and ceaselessly wondering how everything works. My pursuit of web development and software engineering is an extension of these qualities, and I am excited and dedicated to building the best products through these practices.

I took a chance with my education to pursue the unlikely combination of Finance and Biomedical Engineering at the University of Pennsylvania, ultimately discovering a sweetspot between numbers-centered analytical problem-solving, and optimization-based intuitive business strategy.

My career has centered me around data analytics and growth strategy. From customer behavior management at Capital One, to marketing and retail analytics at Oportun, and finally new product and growth initiatives at LendingClub, I have been able to explore a wide variety of roles within the financial services and fintech industry. I'm now ready to step into the next chapter and pursue a career in engineering!

Outside of my career and knowledge pursuits, I focus my time on mental, physical, and social upkeep. Whether it's through enjoying the little victories in board/video games, tapping out on kettlebell workouts, or dragging friends out on Bay Area day trips, I find significant revitalization in life's R&R.
